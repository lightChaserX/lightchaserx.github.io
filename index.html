Zhixiang Wang | 汪智祥

I am a Research Scientist in the Graphics Team at CyberAgent AI Lab, working on computational photography and generative modeling.

I earned my PhD from the University of Tokyo in 2024 under the guidance of Shin'ichi Satoh and with financial supported from MEXT and RIISE. Previously, I completed my Master's degree at National Taiwan University in 2020, where I worked with Yung-Yu Chuang. My research journey has been significantly shaped during my collaboration with Jia-Bin Huang, Yinqiang Zheng, Jian Wang, and Jinwei Gu as well as the internship experiences I had with Shree Nayar's team at Snap Research, and SONY AI.

Feel free to reach out if you're interested in working with me.
