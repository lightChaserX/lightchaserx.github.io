I am a Research Scientist in the Graphics Team at CyberAgent AI Lab. I earned my PhD from the University of Tokyo in 2024 under the guidance of Shin'ichi Satoh. My doctoral studies were supported by the MEXT Scholarship and RIISE. Previously, I completed my Master's degree at National Taiwan University in 2020, where I worked with Yung-Yu Chuang.

My research road has been significantly shaped during my collaboration with Jia-Bin Huang, Yinqiang Zheng, Jian Wang, and Jinwei Gu. I also appreciate the internship experiences I had with Shree Nayar's team at Snap Research, and the DGM team at SONY AI. Before my PhD study, I spent some time at the NII in Japan and NTU in Singapore. These experiences inspired my decision to settle in Tokyo.

My research interest is Computational Photography and Imaging. I am passionate about collaboration and mentorship and have actively worked with research groups led by Yung-Yu Chuang, Yinqiang Zheng, Jian Wang, Zheng Wang, Yu-Lun Liu, and Kaipeng Zhang. Feel free to reach out to me if you are interested in collaborating or chatting about anything.
